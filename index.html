About Our Company

VASILIAS FOROUN TRADING AND PROJECTS (PTY) LTD is a South African black-owned company established in 2021 providing multi-disciplinary industrial and infrastructure services.

Our company focuses on delivering high-quality solutions in construction, industrial services, cleaning, logistics, horticulture, catering and industrial supplies.
